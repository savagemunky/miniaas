About Mini IaaS

Mini Infrastructure as a Service (Mini IaaS) is a Infrastructure as a Service project that is designed to run on low-cost and low-power hardware such as Raspberry Pi's. The idea is not to replace traditional infrastructure, but rather to support and assist it. This is achieved by taking smaller services such as DHCP, DNS, NTP, and domain controller off of primary IaaS solutions and putting them on Mini IaaS.


Other possible use for Mini IaaS include:

  • As a development environment for applications designed for portable or embedded devices
  • As a test environment for applications designed portable or embedded devices
  • As hosting for lightweight web servers
